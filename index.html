Biography

London based sound designer, composer and sound editor with more than 5 years of experience in the music and sound for moving image industries. Primary objective is to bring to life a collaborator's sonic vision, however nuanced. Experienced composer, supervising sound editor, sound designer, ADR recordist/mixer, recording engineer, mixing engineer, mastering engineer, and often times all of the above.

Familiar with a wide variety of professional audio equipment - sound mixers, portable recording devices, microphones, digital control surfaces, multiple DAWs, various recording procedures and techniques. Confident composer for film, animation, and other types of visual media. Skilful in production recording, mixing, editing sound effects and dialogue tracks in Pro Tools. Trained in stereo and surround sound mixing for visual media as well as music. Competent in studio etiquette and able to anticipate and articulate the needs of clients.

Recently, Filip has attained a BA from Unviersity of the Arts London in Sound Arts & Design
