Furniture Work (2017)
in-store soundtrack

Furniture Work is a sound piece composed for synthesizer and voice, an imaginary commission for fantasy in-store music. The intention of the work is to act as background music for browsing a store, by promoting subtle sonic development and structural nuance, to an otherwise non changing but harmonically neutral motif.
